The Wishart Node Process

Step 1

Contact the Wishart Node with your project details, or let us design the project for you.

Step 2

Ship your samples to the TMIC Wishart Node at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, Canada.

Step 3

Once your samples have arrived, the Wishart Node team will begin its quality-controlled analysis according to your project specifications. The results will be compiled into a comprehensive, easy-to-read report and emailed to you.
