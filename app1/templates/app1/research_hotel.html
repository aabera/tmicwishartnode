Research Hotel

In 2011, Dr. Wishart began a training program within TMIC known as the ‘Research Hotel’. The program’s mission is to provide new researchers with access to core facilities, office and bench space, as well as to provide active training and mentoring. National and international visitors wanting to learn more about metabolomics under the supervision of TMIC’s expert staff and scientists are welcome to apply. Please note that the visitors are expected to arrange for their accommodations and cover the costs of their living/travel expenses while visiting. The pins on the map show countries researchers have travelled from to be trained by our metabolomics experts.

Who Can Apply?

Early-career scientists (e.g., graduate students, postdoctoral fellows) in metabolomics, as well as scientists in other fields who want to learn more about metabolomics to apply it to their research, are welcome to apply.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the TMIC website for the application.
  2. Create a research proposal and submit it with the application.

Acceptance Guidelines

  1. Admission criteria is based on the applicant’s accomplishments and research proposal.
  2. Applications will be reviewed within 2-3 weeks of submission.
  3. Successful candidates will be contacted for further administrative requirements and to discuss their research proposal.
  4. Following selection, students/postdocs must provide a letter from their home institution confirming their status.
