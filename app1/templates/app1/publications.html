Publication Policy

Listed below are recent publications written or co-written by Dr. Wishart or someone on the Wishart Node team. Publications, posters, or oral presentations resulting from work performed by The Wishart Node of The Metabolomics Innovation Centre should acknowledge the Node, TMIC and/or the Researcher(s). We may use this information to apply for additional funding to keep our Lab operational and to continue to offer budget-friendly metabolomics analysis. Please keep us informed if you quote our work or use one of the papers for your own research. Search publications by topic or keyword.
